    About Us

    We created Tutorca as a means to help people who needed it and otherwise couldn't get the support they wanted. We ourselves are local A level students in Buckinghamshire and so we know how expensive and inaccessible help can be.

    ​

    We decided to do something and so Tutorca was born. We are all high achieving A level students and for every subject at GCSE where we tutor we received a minimum of a grade nine so you can be sure that we have the knowledge and the skills to help you. 

    ​

    Signed, 

    Michael

     

    How this Works

    Signing up with Tutorca is really simple. All you have to do is go to the booking page and sign up for a session.

    We are currently accommodating years 7-11 for tutoring and years 12-13 for tuition however, this may change. 

    ​

    The sessions work as follows:

    Students can receive one hour of tutoring every week however, tutors can tutor as many hours as they want provided there is availability. Every student will be assigned a tutor who will not change unless any issues arise.

    If any issues arise be they big or small do not hesitate to contact us at tutorcatuition@gmail.com and we will deal with them as promptly as possible. 

    ​

    Currently we are not looking for any tutors however, very soon we will be and so if you're interested send us an email and we'll respond as soon as possible.
